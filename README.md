## Why

You're busy. You have a job, a life, things to do.

But AI moves fast. Every day there's a new tool, a new technique, a launch that changes how people work — and most of it gets buried in Twitter threads, YouTube videos, and Instagram reels you'll never scroll to.

**Sampler fixes that.**

This is a single, curated feed of everything that matters in AI — distilled into quick reads you can scan in minutes. No algorithm. No engagement bait. No 10-minute video for a 2-sentence insight.

Just the signal.

### What you get

- **New launches** the day they drop — not a week later from a newsletter
- **Tools** that are actually useful, not just hyped
- **Prompts & techniques** from people doing real work
- **Resources** you didn't know existed
- **Everything summarized** so you can decide in seconds if it's worth a deeper look

### What you skip

- Scrolling Twitter for hours
- Watching 20-minute YouTube videos for one takeaway
- Asking "what did I miss?" in Discord servers
- FOMO
